    CHARITABLE BARTERING

    Donate items you no longer need to charitable organizations and earn points or rewards on our platform!

    At Ebarter, we believe in giving back to the community while promoting sustainability and social responsibility. With our Charitable Bartering feature, you can make a positive impact by donating gently used items to those in need.

    : Here's how it works :
    1 . Choose items from your inventory that you no longer need or use.
    2 . Find a list of approved charitable organizations partnered with our platform.
    3 . Select the organization you wish to donate to and schedule a pickup or drop-off.
    4 . Receive points or rewards on our platform as a thank-you for your contribution.

    By participating in Charitable Bartering, you not only declutter your space but also support causes that matter to you. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of others while enjoying the benefits of a sustainable and giving community.
